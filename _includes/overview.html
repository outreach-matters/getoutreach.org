Community
Any fedizen - a person or organization with an account on the Fediverse - can join communities on a server that runs Outreach. You select a community based on the causes they support and which are most appealing to you. Don't find a community? No problem, just create your own one and start building. Or even better.. launch your own Outreach server to be in full control. Whether you are a climate change activist, free and open software evangelist, or you want to promote the worker cooperative you are part of. Outreach has got you covered.
